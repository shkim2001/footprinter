What is an environmental footprint?
Area that a defined population / organisation
requires to produce the natural resources it consumes
and to absorb its waste in global hectare (gha)
Why should you be aware of your
environmental footprint?
It helps you identify problems related to business problems
and helps you reduce energy and resource use, saving money.
It helps you meet customer requests for environmental footprint,
taking corporate social responsibility.
Footprint is an online solution that allows
you to calculate your organization’s
environmental footprint for FREE!
